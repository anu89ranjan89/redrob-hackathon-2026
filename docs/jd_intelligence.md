# JD Intelligence Document

## 1. Role Summary
Senior AI Engineer focused on retrieval systems,
ranking systems, semantic matching, embeddings,
production ML systems, and candidate discovery.

The role values practical engineering experience
over keyword matching and prefers candidates with
evidence of building scalable systems.


## 2. Must Have Requirements
- Retrieval Systems
- Ranking Systems
- Embeddings
- Vector Databases
- Python


## 3. Preferred Requirements
- Open Source Contributions
- Learning To Rank
- Product Company Experience


## 4. Negative Signals
- Keyword Stuffing
- Inactive Candidate
- Pure Services Background

## 5. Hidden Recruiter Intent
| JD Says                | Actually Means                  |
| ---------------------- | ------------------------------- |
| Embeddings             | Semantic Candidate Matching     |
| Vector Database        | Retrieval Infrastructure        |
| Retrieval Systems      | Search & Matching Engines       |
| Ranking Systems        | Relevance Optimization          |
| Recommendation Systems | Intelligent Candidate Discovery |
| Product Experience     | Production Scale Engineering    |



## 6. Ideal Candidate Archetype

- 5–9 years of experience
- Product company background
- Experience building retrieval systems
- Experience building ranking/recommendation systems
- Strong Python and ML engineering skills
- Recently active on platform
- High recruiter response rate
- Short to moderate notice period
- Evidence-backed skills rather than keyword lists


## 7. Risk Candidate Archetype

- Large number of AI buzzwords
- Little evidence in work history
- Long inactivity period
- Low recruiter response rate
- Service-only career history
- Inconsistent profile information
