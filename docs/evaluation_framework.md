# Evaluation Framework

## Purpose
Evaluate ranking quality before submission.
---

## Evaluation Goals
1. Technical relevance
2. Recruitability relevance
3. Explanation quality
4. Honeypot detection
5. Ranking consistency
---

## Manual Review Checklist
For every Top 20 candidate:
### Technical Fit
- Relevant experience?
- Relevant projects?
- Relevant skills?
### Recruitability
- Active candidate?
- Good response rate?
- Reasonable notice period?
### Trust
- Verified profile?
- Consistent timeline?
### Risk
- Any honeypot indicators?
---

## Ranking Quality Metrics
### Average Technical Fit
Purpose:
Ensure top candidates are technically strong.
---

### Average Recruitability
Purpose:
Ensure selected candidates are realistically hireable.
---
### Active Candidate Percentage
Purpose:
Measure engagement level.
---
### Honeypot Detection Rate
Purpose:
Measure how many suspicious candidates were filtered.
---
## Explanation Review
Check:
- Contains specific facts
- Connects to JD requirements
- Includes evidence
- Includes concerns when needed
---
## Final Sanity Checks
- Top candidates look reasonable
- No obvious keyword stuffers
- No duplicate candidates
- Scores are sorted correctly


## Benchmark Candidate Review
For Top 10 Candidates:
Record:
- Candidate ID
- Technical Fit Score
- Recruitability Score
- Trust Score
- Activity Score
- Risk Level
- Explanation Quality
Purpose:
Create a benchmark set for comparing future ranking improvements.
